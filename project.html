About ReGive

Transforming lives through the power of giving and community support

Our Mission

ReGive is a NSS initiative by GIET University, Gunupur, Odisha, dedicated to collecting used items from donors and distributing them to those in need within our community.

We believe that every item has value and can make a difference in someone's life. By facilitating the redistribution of goods, we aim to reduce waste while helping those less fortunate.

Our volunteers work tirelessly to collect, sort, and distribute donations to ensure they reach the people who need them most in our local community.
